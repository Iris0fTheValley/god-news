Neighbors reopen the reading room with donated books

Volunteers spent a quiet Saturday sorting shelves and preparing a welcoming corner for local readers.

By the god-news demo editorial team · July 16, 2026

Boxes of donated books arrived throughout the morning, and residents worked together to sort them by reading level and subject. The room will be used for free reading sessions and a weekly book exchange.

Organizers said the most useful part of the project was not the new furniture, but the steady stream of neighbors who offered an hour of their time.
