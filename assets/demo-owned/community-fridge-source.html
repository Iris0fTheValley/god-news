NEIGHBORS · FOOD SHARING

Neighbors keep a community fridge stocked

Fresh donations arrive in small amounts throughout the day, while volunteers check labels, dates and temperatures.

By the god-news demo editorial team · July 16, 2026

The fridge follows a simple rule: anyone may leave food, and anyone who needs it may take it. Volunteers post clear handling guidance beside the door.

Organizers say consistency matters more than large donations. A few items from many households keep the shelves useful.
