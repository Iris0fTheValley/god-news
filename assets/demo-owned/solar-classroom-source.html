Students power a classroom with sunlight

Teachers turned a small rooftop installation into a practical lesson in energy, measurement and teamwork.

By the god-news demo editorial team · July 16, 2026

Students measured the roof, compared panel angles and followed the stored energy from the controller to the classroom lights.

The system is deliberately small, but the teachers plan to keep using its live readings in science and mathematics lessons.
